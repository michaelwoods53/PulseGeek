AI Governance Framework

A structured set of policies, roles, and processes that guide how AI is designed, deployed, and monitored to meet ethical and compliance goals.

In detail

An AI governance framework defines how an organization manages AI risks and responsibilities. It sets principles, roles, approval steps, and documentation requirements across the lifecycle, from data collection to monitoring. Strong frameworks align with external standards, include risk assessments, fairness testing, interpretability expectations, and incident response plans. They also assign accountability for approvals and audits, and require transparent reporting to stakeholders. A practical framework adapts to changing business needs and technology, uses measurable controls, and supports continuous improvement. It helps teams build safer systems and demonstrate responsible practices to customers and regulators.

Synonyms

responsible AI framework, AI policy framework, AI risk management framework

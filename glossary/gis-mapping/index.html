GIS Mapping

Geospatial visualization of lighting assets and performance.

In detail

GIS mapping links poles to coordinates, circuits, and service zones. It helps plan maintenance routes and analyze coverage or dark spots.

Synonyms

asset mapping, geospatial mapping

Abbreviations

GIS

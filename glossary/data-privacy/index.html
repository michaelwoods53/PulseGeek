Data Privacy

Data privacy covers how personal information is collected, stored, and used. Schools must protect student data when adopting AI tools and services.

In detail

Data privacy is the practice of handling personal information responsibly and legally. In education, it includes consent, data minimization, access controls, encryption, and clear retention policies. AI tools may process sensitive student records or behavioral data. Schools should vet vendors, limit data sharing, and explain how data supports learning. Families and students deserve transparency and options to opt out where possible. Good privacy practices build trust and reduce risk.

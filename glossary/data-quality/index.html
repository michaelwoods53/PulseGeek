Data Quality

Data quality measures how fit data is for use. It covers completeness, accuracy, consistency, timeliness, and uniqueness to support strong AI outcomes.

In detail

Data quality is the degree to which data meets requirements for accuracy, completeness, consistency, timeliness, and uniqueness. High data quality improves AI accuracy, reduces rework, and speeds up delivery. Improvement tactics include profiling, validation rules, reference data checks, and monitoring pipelines for drift or breaks. Data quality management is ongoing, with owners accountable and issues tracked to closure. Good metadata and lineage help teams find trusted data quickly.

Synonyms

data integrity, data accuracy

Abbreviations

DQ

Computer Vision

AI that understands images and video. In retail it tracks shelves, shoppers, and queues to improve availability, safety, and service.

In detail

Computer vision is a field of AI that enables systems to interpret visual information from cameras and images. In retail, it detects products on shelves, flags missing items, reads labels, measures facing counts, and tracks shopper flow while respecting privacy policies. Vision can also monitor queues, self-checkout, and store conditions. Combined with analytics, it triggers alerts for restocking, verifies planogram compliance, and informs layout decisions. Computer vision helps reduce labor-intensive audits and creates real-time visibility into store execution.

Synonyms

image recognition, video analytics

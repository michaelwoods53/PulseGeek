Predictive Maintenance

Using data to forecast failures and plan repairs before outages occur.

In detail

Predictive maintenance analyzes sensor data, switching cycles, and voltage anomalies to schedule targeted repairs. It reduces truck rolls and improves service levels.

Synonyms

condition-based maintenance, cbm

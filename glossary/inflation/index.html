Inflation

A sustained rise in prices over time.

In detail

In virtual economies, inflation happens when money enters faster than it leaves, raising prices for items and services. Designers fight it with stronger sinks, scarcity controls, and reward tuning.

Synonyms

price inflation

Related terms

  • Currency Sink
  • Money Supply
  • Price Index

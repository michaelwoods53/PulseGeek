Anomaly Detection

Techniques that find unusual data points or patterns, like odd expenses or duplicate payments, which may signal errors, policy breaks, or fraud risks in finance processes.

In detail

Anomaly detection identifies data points or patterns that do not fit expected behavior. In finance, it flags unusual expenses, duplicate invoices, suspicious journal entries, or sudden balance shifts. Methods range from rule-based checks to machine learning models. Effective programs combine domain thresholds, peer group analysis, and unsupervised techniques. Clear alerting, triage rules, and feedback loops help reduce false positives. Results should be reviewed in dashboards and tied to controls and audit trails.

Synonyms

outlier detection, deviation detection

Telemetry

Event data collected from clients and servers.

In detail

Telemetry captures gameplay events, inputs, and performance signals. Anti-cheat systems use it to compute features, monitor trends, and power heuristics and ML models while respecting privacy and legal limits.

Synonyms

game analytics, event data

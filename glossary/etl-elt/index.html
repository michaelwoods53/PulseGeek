ETL and ELT

Processes that move and transform data for analytics and AI.

In detail

ETL extracts, transforms, and loads data into a warehouse, while ELT loads first and transforms later. Both approaches feed clean, reliable data to AI and BI systems.

Synonyms

data pipelines

Abbreviations

ETL, ELT

Model Governance

Model governance sets controls to develop, validate, deploy, and monitor AI models safely, reducing bias, drift, and compliance risk across the model lifecycle.

In detail

Model governance is a set of standards and processes to manage AI models from design through retirement. It covers documentation, testing, validation, approvals, and monitoring for performance, fairness, robustness, and security. Model governance reduces risk by detecting drift, bias, and data leakage before they harm outcomes. It aligns with regulatory expectations, especially in finance and healthcare, and clarifies roles across data science, risk, and business owners. Clear evidence trails and change logs support audits and continuous improvement.

Synonyms

model risk management, ml governance

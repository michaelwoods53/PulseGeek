Cloud AI

Cloud AI delivers AI features through online services, allowing schools to use powerful models without managing complex infrastructure.

In detail

Cloud AI refers to AI capabilities provided through cloud platforms. Schools can access language, vision, and analytics services through APIs and dashboards. This reduces upfront costs and simplifies updates. However, leaders must review data flows, region controls, and compliance needs before adoption. Strong identity management, encryption, and logging help protect student information. With the right governance, cloud AI can speed innovation while maintaining security and responsible practices.

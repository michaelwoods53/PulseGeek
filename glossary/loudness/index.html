Loudness

A measure of perceived audio level standardized in LUFS.

In detail

Loudness standards help keep mixes consistent across content. Game mixes often target a reasonable LUFS range with enough headroom for transients and dynamic events.

Synonyms

integrated loudness, perceived loudness

Abbreviations

LUFS

False Positive Rate

Share of benign events incorrectly flagged as threats.

In detail

The false positive rate is the ratio of negative samples classified as positive to all actual negatives. Lowering FPR reduces analyst fatigue in SOC settings.

Synonyms

FPR

Abbreviations

FPR

In-Store Analytics

Measuring shopper behavior and store operations using sensors, vision, and sales data. AI turns raw signals into insights for layout, staffing, and merchandising.

In detail

In-store analytics captures and analyzes data inside physical stores. Sources include cameras, Wi-Fi, POS, beacons, and IoT sensors. AI processes these signals to understand traffic, dwell time, conversion, queue lengths, and shelf conditions. Insights guide layout changes, staff scheduling, product placement, and service improvements. When combined with planogram and inventory data, in-store analytics supports rapid issue detection and continuous improvement in execution and customer experience.

Synonyms

store analytics, shopper analytics

Anti-Money Laundering

Policies and analytics to detect and report suspicious activity that hides illegal funds, often using risk scores, alerts, and case reviews.

In detail

Anti-money laundering programs aim to prevent criminals from disguising illicit funds as legitimate income. AML teams monitor transactions, customers, and counterparties for red flags like unusual flows or high-risk jurisdictions. AI can prioritize alerts, detect complex patterns, and reduce false positives. Key elements include risk scoring, ongoing monitoring, KYC processes, and suspicious activity reports. Clear documentation, model governance, and auditability are essential to meet regulatory expectations.

Promotion Uplift

The extra sales driven by a promotion beyond what would have happened anyway. AI estimates uplift and helps choose the best offers and timing.

In detail

Promotion uplift is the incremental sales attributed to a promotion after removing baseline demand. Measuring uplift requires a good baseline forecast and controls for seasonality, cannibalization, and stockouts. AI methods use causal modeling, experiments, and propensity scoring to estimate true lift and profitability. With accurate uplift, retailers can pick better offers, schedule them effectively, and avoid wasteful discounts that do not change customer behavior.

Synonyms

promo lift, incremental sales lift

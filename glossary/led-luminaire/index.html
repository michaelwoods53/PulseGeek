LED Luminaire

A street light fixture that uses light-emitting diodes for illumination.

In detail

An LED luminaire delivers high efficacy, long life, and controllability. In smart deployments, it pairs with controllers and sensors for dimming and remote monitoring to reduce energy and maintenance costs.

Synonyms

led street light, solid-state street light

Abbreviations

LED

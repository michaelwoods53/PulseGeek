Total Cost of Ownership

The full cost of owning a system across its lifecycle.

In detail

Total cost of ownership adds up acquisition, integration, training, operations, and maintenance. In ai programs, tco also includes data labeling, evaluation, monitoring, and periodic model updates.

Synonyms

lifecycle cost

Abbreviations

TCO

Related terms

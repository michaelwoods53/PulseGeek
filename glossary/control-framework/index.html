Control Framework

Policies and procedures that help keep financial data accurate, secure, and compliant when AI is used in processes.

In detail

A control framework sets the structure for how finance teams manage risk, accuracy, and compliance. It includes segregation of duties, approvals, exception handling, documentation, and monitoring. When AI is added to workflows, the framework should cover model validation, data quality checks, alert triage, and audit trails. Clear ownership and periodic testing ensure controls remain effective as systems change.

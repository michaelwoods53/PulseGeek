Assortment Optimization

Choosing the right mix of products for each store or channel to meet demand and improve margins. AI analyzes sales, basket patterns, and space limits to recommend the best lineup.

In detail

Assortment optimization determines the ideal set of products to carry by store, region, or channel. It balances customer preferences, local demand, and physical or digital space constraints with financial goals like revenue and margin. AI enhances this process by analyzing detailed sales histories, cross-elasticities, and basket affinities. It can simulate changes, test new items, and measure the impact of delisting underperformers. With AI, retailers align assortments with demand signals, reduce out-of-stocks, and improve category performance.

Synonyms

range optimization, category assortment

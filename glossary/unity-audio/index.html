Unity Audio

Built-in audio features in the Unity engine for playback, mixing, and effects.

In detail

Unity Audio provides components and APIs for sound playback, 3D spatialization, mixing, effects, and scripting. It can be used alone or with middleware and is often combined with packages and plugins for advanced spatial audio needs.

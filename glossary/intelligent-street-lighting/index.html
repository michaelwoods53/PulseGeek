Intelligent Street Lighting

Networked outdoor lighting that adjusts brightness and reports status in real time.

In detail

Intelligent street lighting uses LED luminaires, sensors, and connected controllers to modulate light levels, report outages, and support safety. Systems integrate with management software to automate schedules, optimize energy, and provide analytics for maintenance and planning.

Synonyms

smart street lighting, connected street lighting

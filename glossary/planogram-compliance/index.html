Planogram Compliance

Measuring how well in-store shelves match the approved planogram. AI and computer vision can detect exceptions and guide quick fixes.

In detail

Planogram compliance measures how closely the actual shelf layout in a store matches the approved planogram. Retailers monitor compliance to protect sales, ensure brand standards, and simplify audits. Traditionally, compliance checks are manual and time consuming. AI and computer vision automate this by analyzing shelf images, detecting missing facings, misplaced items, or incorrect pricing labels. Real-time alerts help teams resolve issues faster. Improved compliance increases sales, reduces shopper frustration, and supports suppliers’ trade agreements.

Synonyms

shelf compliance, merchandising compliance

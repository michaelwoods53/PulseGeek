Phishing

A social engineering attack that tricks people into revealing information or installing malware. AI models spot phishing by analyzing text, headers, and URLs.

In detail

Phishing uses deceptive messages or sites to steal credentials, money, or access. Attackers often impersonate trusted brands and urge quick action. AI improves defenses by classifying content, checking sender authenticity, and evaluating URLs. It also helps find spear phishing that targets specific people using tailored messages and language cues.

Synonyms

email scam

Ethical AI

Principles and practices that reduce bias, protect privacy, and make AI more fair and transparent.

In detail

Ethical AI is the set of principles and methods used to ensure AI systems are fair, transparent, and respectful of privacy. It includes bias testing, explainability, data minimization, and consent. Finance teams rely on ethical AI to avoid harmful outcomes and meet regulatory expectations. Clear policies, diverse testing data, and human oversight help maintain fairness and trust in automated decisions.

Synonyms

responsible ai, trustworthy ai

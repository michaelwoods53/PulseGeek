Chokepoint

A narrow space that controls movement between areas.

In detail

Chokepoints limit approach vectors and concentrate players or AI into predictable routes. Used carefully, they balance encounters and frame tactical choices without promoting camping.

Synonyms

bottleneck

Abbreviations

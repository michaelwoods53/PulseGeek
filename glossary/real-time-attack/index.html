Real Time Attack

Timing method that measures wall-clock time of the run.

In detail

Real Time Attack measures the continuous elapsed time from start to end under defined start/stop rules. RTA may include loads unless the category mandates load removal.

Synonyms

real-time

Abbreviations

RTA

Related terms

  • In-Game Time
  • Loadless Time
  • Retiming

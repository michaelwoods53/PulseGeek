Jitter

Variation in latency over time that can cause stutter and rubberbanding.

In detail

Jitter is the fluctuation of latency between packets. Even with low average ping, high jitter can create inconsistent gameplay, missed shots, and rubberbanding.

Synonyms

latency variation, delay variation

Related terms

  • Network Latency
  • Packet Loss
  • Bufferbloat

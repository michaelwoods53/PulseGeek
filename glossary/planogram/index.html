Planogram

A diagram showing where products should be placed on shelves to increase sales and improve shopper experience. AI helps optimize and check compliance automatically.

In detail

A planogram is a visual diagram that shows how products should be arranged on shelves, endcaps, and displays. It aims to improve visibility, make shopping easier, and boost sales. Traditional planograms are created by merchandisers using rules and past results. AI-driven planograms use sales data, shopper behavior, and store constraints to recommend the best layout. Computer vision can verify compliance from shelf images, reducing manual audits and speeding resets. Optimized planograms lead to higher conversion, better space use, and more consistent execution.

Synonyms

shelf plan, pogrames

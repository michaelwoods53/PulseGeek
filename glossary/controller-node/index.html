Controller Node

A device mounted on a luminaire to control dimming and connect to a network.

In detail

Controller nodes execute schedules, receive sensor inputs, and communicate status to a central platform using protocols like Zigbee, LoRaWAN, NB-IoT, or cellular. They enable adaptive lighting and remote diagnostics.

Synonyms

lighting controller, street light controller

Related terms

  • Zigbee
  • LoRaWAN
  • NB-IoT

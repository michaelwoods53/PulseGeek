Post-Processing

Effects applied after rendering the scene.

In detail

Post-processing includes motion blur, bloom, chromatic aberration, and other effects that change the final image, often with small but cumulative performance costs.

Synonyms

post effects

Related terms

  • Motion Blur
  • Depth of Field

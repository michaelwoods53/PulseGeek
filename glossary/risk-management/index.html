Risk Management

Risk management identifies, assesses, and controls potential harms. For AI, it covers model failures, bias, security, compliance, and operational risks.

In detail

Risk management is the process of finding and addressing threats that could harm objectives. In AI, risks include data leakage, biased outcomes, poor accuracy, model drift, prompt injection, and regulatory noncompliance. Practical controls include access management, testing, monitoring, documentation, and incident response. Clear ownership, thresholds, and playbooks help teams react quickly. Integrating risk reviews into design and deployment reduces surprises and supports audit requirements.

Synonyms

risk control, risk mitigation

Abbreviations

RM

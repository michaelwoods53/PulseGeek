Prompt Engineering

The practice of designing inputs that guide AI models to produce accurate, useful, and safe outputs for a task, using patterns, examples, constraints, and evaluation to improve results.

In detail

Prompt Engineering is the structured process of creating inputs that steer AI systems toward reliable and helpful outputs. It blends clear instructions, role framing, constraints, and examples to reduce ambiguity and improve results. Practitioners use patterns like chain-of-thought and few-shot prompting, and they iterate with test sets, rubrics, and feedback. The goal is to make outputs consistent, safe, and aligned with business or creative needs. Good prompt engineering includes documentation, versioning, and governance so teams can reuse and improve prompts over time without relying on a single tool or vendor.

Synonyms

prompt design, prompt crafting, prompt writing

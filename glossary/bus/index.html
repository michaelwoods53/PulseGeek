Bus

A routing path in a mixer that groups sounds for processing and level control.

In detail

Buses organize audio into categories like music, SFX, and VO, enabling shared processing, metering, and ducking. Bus hierarchies help control large mixes and manage headroom and loudness.

Synonyms

mix bus, audio bus

Abbreviations

BUS

Know Your Customer

Processes to verify identity and assess customer risk, often used with AML programs and onboarding controls.

In detail

Know Your Customer is a set of checks that verify the identity of customers and assess their risk before and during a business relationship. It includes document verification, watchlist screening, and ongoing monitoring. AI can speed checks and reduce errors, while risk scoring and reviews catch potential issues. KYC supports AML and fraud controls and requires strong documentation and auditability.

customer due diligence

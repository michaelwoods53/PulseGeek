Chatbot

A chatbot is a software agent that interacts through text or voice to answer questions, guide tasks, or provide tutoring and support.

In detail

A chatbot is an application that communicates with users via text or voice. Many modern chatbots use natural language processing and sometimes generative AI to understand intent and produce helpful responses. In education, chatbots can handle FAQs, provide study tips, give instant feedback, and assist with language practice. Effective chatbots are trained on accurate content, monitor user satisfaction, and hand off to humans when needed. Schools should consider privacy, data retention, and accessibility when deploying chatbots.

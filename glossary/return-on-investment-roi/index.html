ROI (Return on Investment)

A measure of financial gain relative to cost.

In detail

ROI quantifies the net benefit of AI projects compared to their costs, including development, data, and change management. It helps prioritize use cases and track realized value.

Synonyms

return on capital

Abbreviations

ROI

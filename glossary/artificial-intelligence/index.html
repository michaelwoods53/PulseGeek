Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is the field of building computer systems that can perform tasks that usually require human thinking, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, and making decisions.

In detail

Artificial intelligence is a branch of computer science focused on creating systems that can perform tasks associated with human intelligence. These tasks include perception, language understanding, reasoning, learning, and decision-making. AI methods range from simple rules to data-driven models that learn from examples. In education, AI can support tutoring, feedback, assessment, content creation, and administrative tasks. Responsible use includes addressing data privacy, transparency, bias, and safety. AI systems work best when guided by clear goals, quality data, and human oversight to ensure fairness and helpful outcomes for learners and teachers.

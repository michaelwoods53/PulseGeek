Speculation

Buying items expecting future price changes.

In detail

Speculation can create bubbles and volatility in virtual markets. Developers may use taxes, listing limits, or supply changes to curb excessive swings.

Synonyms

market speculation

Related terms

  • Arbitrage
  • Price Index
  • Dynamic Pricing

Demand Sensing

Capturing short-term demand signals from POS, web traffic, and events to adjust forecasts quickly. AI improves responsiveness and reduces stockouts.

In detail

Demand sensing focuses on near-term signals to update forecasts rapidly. It uses POS data, online activity, weather, local events, and promotions to detect changes in demand as they happen. AI models process these signals and adjust item-store forecasts, enabling better replenishment and labor planning. Demand sensing complements longer-horizon planning by keeping execution aligned with real-world conditions, reducing lost sales and excess stock.

Synonyms

near-term forecasting, short-term demand signal

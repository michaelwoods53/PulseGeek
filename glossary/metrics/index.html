Metrics

Quantitative measures to judge generated content.

In detail

Metrics track properties like path length, room variety, loot fairness, and failure rates. Designers use dashboards and thresholds to guide tuning and detect regressions.

Synonyms

evaluation metrics

Related terms

  • Content Variability

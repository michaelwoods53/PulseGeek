Gain Staging

Setting signal levels to avoid noise and clipping across the chain.

In detail

Proper gain staging maintains consistent levels from asset import through buses and effects. It preserves headroom and supports predictable ducking and snapshot changes.

Synonyms

level staging

Abbreviations

GAIN

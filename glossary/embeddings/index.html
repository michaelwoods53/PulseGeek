Embeddings

Numeric vectors that capture meaning or behavior of items like words, APIs, or opcodes. They help models generalize and compare similar items.

In detail

Embeddings map items to dense vectors so that similar items lie close together in the vector space. In text, word and sentence embeddings capture semantics. In security, embeddings can represent API calls, opcodes, URLs, or processes. They support clustering, classification, and retrieval, improving accuracy and making downstream models more data-efficient.

Synonyms

vector representations

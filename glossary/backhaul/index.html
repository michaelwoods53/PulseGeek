Backhaul

The connection from field devices or gateways to core networks.

In detail

Backhaul options include cellular, fiber, and fixed wireless. Reliability and bandwidth influence monitoring frequency, firmware updates, and video sensor support.

Synonyms

network backhaul, uplink

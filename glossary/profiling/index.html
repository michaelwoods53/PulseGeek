Profiling

Measuring performance, voice counts, and routing in real time.

In detail

Audio profiling tools show CPU usage, voice limits, bus meters, and effect costs to identify bottlenecks. They guide optimization and verify that mixes behave as designed in gameplay.

Synonyms

audio profiling, performance profiling

Abbreviations

PROF

Related terms

  • Memory Budget
  • Voice Stealing
  • Mixer

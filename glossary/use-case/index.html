Use Case

A defined business problem where AI can deliver value.

In detail

A Use Case describes objectives, inputs, users, workflows, KPIs, and constraints. Good use cases match data readiness and clear ROI potential.

Synonyms

business case

Related terms

  • KPI
  • Data Readiness
  • Return on Investment

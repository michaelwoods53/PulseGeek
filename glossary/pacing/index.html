Pacing

The rise and fall of intensity over time.

In detail

Pacing balances high-intensity moments with downtime to prevent fatigue and maintain interest. Designers shape pacing with encounter density, traversal, and narrative beats.

Synonyms

tempo, rhythm

Abbreviations

PAC

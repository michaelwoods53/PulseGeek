Snapshot

A stored mixer state that can be recalled to change the mix instantly.

In detail

Snapshots capture routing, levels, and effect settings for a mixer and can be transitioned or blended at runtime. They are used to change priorities during dialogue, combat, or cutscenes.

Synonyms

mixer snapshot, mix state

Abbreviations

SNAP

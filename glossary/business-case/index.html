Business Case

A structured argument that explains why a project should be funded.

In detail

A business case outlines goals, expected value, costs, risks, and success metrics. For AI, it links use cases to measurable outcomes and defines milestones, data needs, and adoption plans.

Synonyms

value case, investment case

  • Total Cost of Ownership

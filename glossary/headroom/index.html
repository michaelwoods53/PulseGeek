Headroom

The margin between nominal level and clipping.

In detail

Headroom protects against distortion when many sounds play at once. Proper gain staging and bus management preserve headroom in unpredictable gameplay moments.

Synonyms

level headroom

Abbreviations

HDRM

Related terms

  • Loudness
  • Dynamic Range
  • Gain Staging

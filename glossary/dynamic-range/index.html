Dynamic Range

The difference between the quietest and loudest parts of audio.

In detail

Dynamic range management ensures clarity in quiet scenes and prevents fatigue in loud ones. Techniques include compression, limiting, and state-based or snapshot-driven mixes.

Synonyms

audio dynamics

Abbreviations

DR

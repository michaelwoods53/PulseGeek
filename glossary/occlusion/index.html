Occlusion

Reduction of sound when blocked by objects or walls.

In detail

Occlusion models how geometry blocks and filters sound. Implementations often adjust volume and EQ based on raycasts, portals, or room systems to match player expectations.

Synonyms

audio occlusion

Abbreviations

OCC

  • Attenuation

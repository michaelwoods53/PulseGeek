KPI

A metric that tracks progress toward a goal.

In detail

KPIs measure the outcomes that matter for AI projects, such as conversion rate, handle time, or forecast accuracy. Clear KPIs help teams prove value and guide improvements.

Synonyms

key performance indicator

Abbreviations

KPI

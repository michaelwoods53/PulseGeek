Packet Loss

Packets that never reach their destination, causing stutter or disconnects.

In detail

Packet loss occurs when data is dropped due to congestion, interference, or faulty links. In games it causes rubberbanding, delayed actions, or complete disconnects.

Synonyms

lost packets

Related terms

  • Jitter
  • Bufferbloat
  • UDP

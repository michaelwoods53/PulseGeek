Branching Narrative

A story structure where player choices lead to different paths and outcomes.

In detail

Branching narrative is a design where decisions split the story into multiple paths, often tracked with variables and flags. It enables replayability and consequence-driven storytelling, requiring careful scope control.

Synonyms

nonlinear story, branching story

Related terms

  • Dialogue Tree
  • Variables and Flags

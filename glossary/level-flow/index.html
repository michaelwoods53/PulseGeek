Level Flow

The intended path and pacing through a level.

In detail

Level flow describes how players move from beat to beat, including choices, loops, and backtracking. Strong flow aligns challenge, guidance, and rewards to keep players engaged.

Synonyms

flow, player path

Abbreviations

FLW

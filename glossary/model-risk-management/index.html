Model Risk Management

Practices that ensure models are accurate, explainable, and controlled through validation, monitoring, and documentation.

In detail

Model risk management is a set of practices to reduce the chance that models cause errors or losses. It covers model inventory, development standards, validation, testing, and ongoing monitoring. Finance teams document assumptions, data sources, performance metrics, and change logs. Independent review checks bias, explainability, and stability. Strong MRM aligns with regulations and supports audits and stakeholder trust.

Synonyms

mrm, model governance

Abbreviations

MRM

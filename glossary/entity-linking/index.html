Entity Linking

The process of connecting different mentions of the same real-world entity across sources. It improves threat intelligence by merging duplicate indicators and names.

In detail

Entity linking identifies when different strings refer to the same entity and attaches them to a consistent identity. In threat intelligence, it connects varied references to domains, malware families, or actor names across reports and feeds. Models use similarity, context, and knowledge bases. Better linking creates cleaner graphs, improves enrichment, and reduces duplicated alerts.

Synonyms

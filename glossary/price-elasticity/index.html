Price Elasticity

How demand changes when price changes. AI estimates elasticity by item, store, and time to guide smarter pricing and promotions.

In detail

Price elasticity measures the responsiveness of demand to changes in price. In retail, elasticity varies by product, store, season, and competitive context. AI estimates elasticity using historical sales, prices, promotions, and external data. It can capture cross-elasticities across related products and detect nonlinear effects that simple models miss. Knowing elasticity helps retailers set optimal base prices, plan discounts, and maximize profit without harming customer value perception.

Synonyms

demand elasticity, price sensitivity

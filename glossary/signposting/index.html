Signposting

Visual cues that indicate where to go or interact.

In detail

Signposting includes lighting, color, motion, and landmarks that improve readability. Effective signposting reduces confusion while preserving player agency.

Synonyms

wayfinding, guidance cues

Abbreviations

SIGN

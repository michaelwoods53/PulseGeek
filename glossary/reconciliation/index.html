Reconciliation

Matching transactions across ledgers, statements, and systems to ensure balances agree and errors are resolved quickly.

In detail

Reconciliation compares records across systems, such as bank statements, subledgers, and the general ledger, to confirm balances and activity match. AI speeds matching by using fuzzy logic, embeddings, and rules to link items with different formats or descriptions. It flags exceptions for review, supports attachments, and maintains an audit trail. Effective reconciliation reduces close time, improves accuracy, and supports stronger financial controls.

Synonyms

account reconciliation, bank reconciliation

Abbreviations

RECON

Backtesting

Testing a model or strategy on historical data.

In detail

Backtesting evaluates how a model or trading strategy would have performed using past data. It helps calibrate thresholds, assess risk, and avoid overfitting before production use.

Synonyms

historical simulation

Related terms

  • Model Risk Management
  • Portfolio Risk

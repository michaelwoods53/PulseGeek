Knowledge Graph

A knowledge graph organizes entities and relationships in a connected structure, improving search, reasoning, and data integration for AI applications.

In detail

A knowledge graph represents real-world entities and the relationships between them in a structured, connected format. It unifies data from multiple sources with shared meanings, which helps AI systems retrieve and reason over facts. In enterprises, knowledge graphs power search, recommendations, data lineage, and compliance. They complement vector databases by providing precise, explainable links that support trustworthy answers and auditability.

Synonyms

enterprise graph, semantic graph

Abbreviations

KG

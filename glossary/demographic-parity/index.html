Demographic Parity

A fairness criterion that aims for equal positive outcome rates across groups, regardless of true labels or ground truth distributions.

In detail

Demographic parity is a fairness goal where an AI system produces positive outcomes at similar rates for different groups. It focuses on the proportion selected or approved, not the model’s accuracy per group. While it can improve access and representation, it may conflict with other goals like equalized odds or overall accuracy. Teams use demographic parity when equal opportunity to be selected is the priority. It should be applied with care, documented trade-offs, and regular monitoring to ensure it aligns with policy, product goals, and stakeholder expectations.

Synonyms

statistical parity, selection rate parity

Accounts Payable Automation

Using AI and workflows to capture invoices, match POs, route approvals, and pay vendors faster with fewer errors.

In detail

Accounts payable automation applies AI and workflow tools to digitize invoice capture, three-way matching, approvals, and payments. Core features include document extraction, validation rules, duplicate detection, exception handling, and integration with ERP ledgers. Dashboards track cycle time, first-pass yield, and discount capture. Strong controls help prevent duplicate or fraudulent payments, while audit logs support compliance. Human review is used for exceptions and continuous improvement.

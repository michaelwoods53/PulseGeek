Data Readiness

How fit your data is for AI and analytics tasks.

In detail

Data readiness measures completeness, accuracy, timeliness, and consistency of data for modeling and decision-making. Good readiness reduces model errors, speeds deployment, and improves ROI from AI projects.

Synonyms

Data Quality

Data Governance

Data governance sets policies and roles to manage data quality, access, privacy, and lifecycle, ensuring data is usable, compliant, and secure for AI projects.

In detail

Data governance defines how an organization manages data to ensure quality, security, privacy, and compliance. It includes roles, standards, and processes for cataloging data, setting access controls, tracking lineage, and resolving issues. Strong governance helps AI teams find trustworthy data, reduce risk, and comply with regulations. It also aligns stakeholders by clarifying ownership, definitions, and usage rules. Governance should be practical, with clear metrics and collaboration between data, security, and business teams.

Synonyms

data stewardship, data management policy

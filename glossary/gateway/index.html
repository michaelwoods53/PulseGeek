Gateway

A device that bridges local controllers to the cloud or CMS.

In detail

Gateways aggregate traffic from controllers using mesh or LPWAN and forward data over cellular, fiber, or Ethernet. Placement affects latency and coverage.

Synonyms

lighting gateway, field gateway

Related terms

  • LoRaWAN
  • Zigbee
  • Central Management System

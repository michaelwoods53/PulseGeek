Document Intelligence

AI systems that read and structure data from documents.

In detail

Document intelligence extracts fields from invoices, receipts, and contracts. It validates data, flags exceptions, and posts entries to ERP systems with audit trails.

Synonyms

intelligent document processing

Abbreviations

IDP

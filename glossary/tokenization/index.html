Tokenization

Splitting text into smaller pieces, like words or subwords, for modeling. It supports NLP tasks such as phishing classification and entity extraction from reports.

In detail

Tokenization is the process of breaking text into meaningful units, such as words, subwords, or characters, that models can process. Choices like whitespace, byte-pair encoding, or wordpiece affect vocabulary size, robustness to typos, and handling of rare terms. In security, good tokenization improves phishing classifiers and entity extraction from threat reports with noisy, mixed-case text and embedded URLs.

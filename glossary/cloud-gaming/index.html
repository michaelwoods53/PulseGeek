Cloud Gaming

Playing games rendered on remote servers and streamed to your device.

In detail

Cloud gaming runs the game on a remote server and streams video frames to the player while sending inputs back over the network. Performance depends on latency, bitrate, and stability rather than local GPU power.

Synonyms

game streaming, remote gaming

AI Readiness

AI readiness measures how prepared an organization is to adopt AI, including strategy, data quality, talent, technology, and governance foundations.

In detail

AI readiness is an assessment of an organization’s ability to adopt AI successfully. It examines business strategy alignment, use case prioritization, data quality and access, required skills, platform capabilities, and governance. A readiness scorecard helps teams spot gaps, plan investments, and sequence pilots. Improving readiness reduces project risk and speeds time to value. It also builds confidence among stakeholders by showing a clear path from experiment to scaled impact.

Synonyms

ai maturity, ai preparedness

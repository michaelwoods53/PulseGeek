ROC AUC

A measure of ranking quality across thresholds.

In detail

ROC AUC summarizes how well a model separates positive and negative classes across thresholds by plotting true positive rate vs. false positive rate.

Synonyms

Area Under ROC

Abbreviations

AUC

Related terms

  • Confusion Matrix
  • Precision-Recall Curve
  • Cross-Validation

Optical Character Recognition

Technology that converts images and PDFs of text into machine-readable data, enabling automated document workflows.

In detail

Optical character recognition transforms text in images or PDFs into digital characters that systems can process. For finance, OCR is used to read invoices, receipts, and statements. Modern systems combine OCR with layout detection and AI-based post-processing to improve accuracy and handle noisy scans. Confidence scores determine when to route items to human review, reducing errors while speeding up operations.

Synonyms

text recognition, ocr scanning

Abbreviations

OCR

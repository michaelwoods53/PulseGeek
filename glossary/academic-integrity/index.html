Academic Integrity

Academic integrity means being honest in schoolwork, giving credit, and following rules. It includes responsible use of AI tools and proper citation.

In detail

Academic integrity involves honesty, fairness, and responsibility in learning and research. As AI tools become common, integrity requires clear guidance on what kinds of assistance are allowed, how to cite AI-generated text, and how to demonstrate original thinking. Educators can design assessments that emphasize process, reflection, and collaboration. Tools that detect similarity should be used carefully, with opportunities for students to explain their work and correct mistakes.

Synonyms

honor code, scholarly honesty

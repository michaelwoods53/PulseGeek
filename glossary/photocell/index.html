Photocell

A light sensor that turns fixtures on at dusk and off at dawn.

In detail

Photocells measure ambient light to automate on/off behavior. In smart systems they can work with schedules to reduce switching cycles and improve energy performance.

Synonyms

lux sensor, daylight sensor

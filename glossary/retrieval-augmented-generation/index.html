Retrieval-Augmented Generation

A technique that fetches relevant documents and adds them to the prompt, helping the model generate grounded, up-to-date, and verifiable responses.

In detail

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines search or vector retrieval with generation. The system finds relevant passages from a knowledge base and injects them into the prompt so the model can cite and reason over accurate information. This reduces hallucinations and keeps answers aligned with current facts. Effective RAG systems include chunking strategies, relevance thresholds, citation rules, and guardrails to prevent sensitive data leakage. Teams should measure grounding quality with rubrics and maintain versioned indexes to track changes over time.

Synonyms

knowledge-grounded generation, context-augmented generation

Abbreviations

RAG

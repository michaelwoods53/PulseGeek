ISO 15118 Vehicle-to-Grid Communication Interface

A standard for secure digital communication between EVs and charging stations, enabling Plug and Charge, contract handling, smart charging, and V2G energy services with robust authentication and certificate management.

In detail

ISO 15118 defines a secure, digital communication interface between electric vehicles and charging stations. It supports automatic authentication and authorization using digital certificates, enabling Plug and Charge so drivers can start sessions without cards or apps. The standard covers message flows for contract handling, tariff and schedule exchange, smart charging profiles, and bidirectional power use cases such as vehicle-to-grid. ISO 15118 includes parts for AC and DC charging and complements OCPP, which connects the station to the backend system. Strong cryptography and certificate provisioning are central to its trust model. By enabling standardized negotiation of energy services and pricing, ISO 15118 helps aggregators and utilities align EV charging with grid constraints and renewable output. It also supports future-ready features like higher power levels, load shaping, and coordinated charging across fleets, improving user experience and grid integration.

Synonyms

Plug and Charge standard, EV to EVSE communication, V2G communication interface

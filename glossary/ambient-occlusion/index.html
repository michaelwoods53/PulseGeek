Ambient Occlusion

Shadowing that adds depth in creases and corners.

In detail

Ambient occlusion approximates how ambient light is blocked by nearby geometry, enhancing depth at a performance cost that varies by method.

Synonyms

ssao, hbao

Abbreviations

AO

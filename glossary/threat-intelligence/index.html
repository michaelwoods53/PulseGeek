Threat Intelligence

Curated information about adversaries, indicators, and tactics that helps defenders prioritize risks. AI enriches TI by clustering indicators and extracting entities from reports.

In detail

Threat intelligence is structured and unstructured information about cyber threats, including attacker groups, tools, infrastructure, and indicators of compromise. It helps security teams understand context and anticipate risks. AI adds value by extracting entities, normalizing noisy data, clustering related indicators, and linking signals across sources. Good TI enrichment improves triage, reduces noise, and supports more accurate detections across security tools.

Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing enables computers to understand, generate, and analyze human language, supporting chatbots, search, summarization, and document processing in business workflows.

In detail

Natural language processing is a branch of AI that focuses on working with human language in text and speech. It covers tasks like classification, entity extraction, sentiment analysis, summarization, translation, and conversation. NLP powers chatbots, knowledge assistants, document processing, and customer feedback analysis. Business adoption depends on high-quality domain data, evaluation against clear accuracy targets, and safeguards for privacy and security. Combined with retrieval and structured data, NLP can unlock insights and speed up knowledge work.

language understanding, text analytics

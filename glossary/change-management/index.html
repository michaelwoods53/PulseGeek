Change Management

Change management helps people adopt new ways of working. For AI, it aligns roles, training, communication, and incentives to support safe, lasting adoption.

In detail

Change management is a structured approach that prepares, equips, and supports people through transitions. AI programs often change workflows, responsibilities, and decision rights. Effective change plans include stakeholder mapping, training, communications, and updated performance measures. Leaders should involve end users early, address concerns about job impact, and show clear benefits. Pairing change management with pilot scorecards and playbooks helps scale successful approaches across teams.

Synonyms

organizational change, adoption management

Abbreviations

CM

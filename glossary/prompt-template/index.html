Prompt Template

A reusable structure for prompts with placeholders for variables, examples, and constraints, designed to produce consistent outputs across similar tasks.

In detail

A Prompt Template is a standardized layout for instructions, examples, and constraints that can be reused across tasks. Templates reduce guesswork and make it easier for teams to scale prompt work. They often include placeholders for inputs, guidance on tone and format, and optional chain-of-thought or evaluation steps. Good templates are versioned, documented, and tested with sample datasets. They support governance by making changes traceable and by enabling rapid comparisons across template variants.

Synonyms

prompt pattern, prompt framework

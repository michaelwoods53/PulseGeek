Influence Map

A scalar field representing attraction and repulsion across space.

In detail

Influence maps encode values like danger, control, or resource desirability. Agents read local gradients to make decisions, combine goals, and bias path choices.

Synonyms

potential field, heatmap

Related terms

  • Flow Field
  • Cost Field
  • Heuristic Function

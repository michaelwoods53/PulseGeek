Key Performance Indicator

A measurable value that shows how well a goal is being achieved.

In detail

Key performance indicators track outcomes like revenue, conversion, cycle time, or error rates. For ai projects, kpis align models with business value, including accuracy, adoption, cost savings, and time to value.

Synonyms

performance metric

Abbreviations

KPI

Audit Trail

A detailed record of actions and changes, showing who did what and when, so reviews and compliance checks are possible.

In detail

An audit trail tracks user actions, data changes, and model updates. In finance AI systems, it should log data sources, training runs, model versions, alerts, and resolution steps. Strong logging supports accountability, faster investigations, and regulatory reviews. Good practice includes immutable storage, time stamps, and role-based access. Clear trails help teams prove that controls were applied consistently and that exceptions were handled correctly.

Synonyms

activity log, change log

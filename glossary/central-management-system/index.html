Central Management System

Software that monitors and controls smart street lights at scale.

In detail

A central management system provides dashboards, mapping, alarms, and APIs to manage assets and schedules. It aggregates performance data for analytics, maintenance planning, and reporting.

Synonyms

street light management system, lighting cms

Abbreviations

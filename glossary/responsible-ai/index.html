Responsible AI

Responsible AI means building and using AI systems that are safe, fair, transparent, and aligned with human values, with checks and accountability.

In detail

Responsible AI is the practice of developing and deploying AI systems with strong safeguards. It includes risk assessment, human oversight, clear documentation, and continuous monitoring for bias, drift, and misuse. In education, responsible AI ensures tools support learning objectives, protect students, and respect differences. It also means training teachers and students to use AI thoughtfully. When schools adopt responsible AI, they set expectations for behavior, reporting issues, and improving systems over time.

Synonyms

trustworthy ai, safe ai

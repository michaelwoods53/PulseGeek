Mixer

The system that routes, balances, and processes audio signals.

In detail

A mixer organizes buses, effects, and levels, with scenes or snapshots to adapt to gameplay. It is central to implementing ducking, priorities, and headroom strategies.

Synonyms

audio mixer, game mixer

Abbreviations

MIX

Cover

Geometry that protects players from enemy fire or vision.

In detail

Cover elements like walls, pillars, and props break sightlines, enabling repositioning and fair combat. Consistent spacing and readability are key to good cover design.

Synonyms

hard cover, soft cover

Abbreviations

CVR

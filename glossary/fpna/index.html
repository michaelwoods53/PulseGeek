FP&A

A function that plans budgets, forecasts, and performance reporting.

In detail

FP&A teams build budgets and forecasts, perform variance analysis, and support decision making. AI helps automate data preparation, scenario modeling, and anomaly alerts for plan vs. actuals.

Synonyms

financial planning and analysis

Abbreviations

FP&A

Heatmap

A visual map showing where shoppers spend time or move in a store. AI uses sensors and vision to reveal hot and cold zones for layout and staffing decisions.

In detail

A heatmap is a visual representation of store activity that highlights areas with high or low customer attention. Data can come from cameras, Wi-Fi, beacons, or other sensors. AI processes these signals to detect foot traffic patterns, dwell time, and conversion by area. Retailers use heatmaps to improve layouts, place promotions, and schedule staff more effectively. Ongoing analysis uncovers bottlenecks and opportunities that are hard to see with manual observation alone, supporting continuous operational improvement.

Synonyms

store traffic heatmap, dwell heatmap

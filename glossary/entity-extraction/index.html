Entity Extraction

AI that identifies key fields like vendor names, invoice numbers, dates, and amounts from documents to enable automation.

In detail

Entity extraction finds and labels meaningful items in text and documents, such as names, IDs, dates, and amounts. In finance, it powers invoice capture, purchase order matching, and statement parsing. Models combine layout analysis with language patterns and are often paired with confidence scores and human review. Accurate extraction enables straight-through processing and more reliable reconciliation and reporting.

Synonyms

information extraction, named entity recognition

Abbreviations

EE, NER

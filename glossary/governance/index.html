Governance

Policies and roles that guide how AI is built, used, and monitored to stay safe, fair, and compliant.

In detail

Governance defines responsibilities, policies, and standards for AI systems and data. It covers privacy, security, fairness, documentation, and lifecycle management. In finance, governance links model risk, controls, and regulatory needs. Effective governance includes training, role-based access, change management, incident response, and periodic reviews. It enables consistent decisions and builds trust with stakeholders and regulators.

Synonyms

ai governance, data governance

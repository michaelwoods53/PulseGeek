AI Adoption Roadmap

A phased plan to pilot, scale, and govern AI in the business.

In detail

An AI adoption roadmap prioritizes use cases, defines data readiness tasks, sets ROI targets, and outlines change management activities. It sequences pilots and scale-up with clear metrics and timelines.

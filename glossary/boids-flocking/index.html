Boids

A flocking model using cohesion, alignment, and separation forces.

In detail

Boids simulate group motion by pulling agents toward neighbors, aligning headings, and maintaining distance. With obstacle avoidance and goal following, it creates lifelike schools, flocks, or crowds.

Synonyms

flocking model

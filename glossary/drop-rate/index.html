Drop Rate

The probability that a specific item or item tier appears after an event.

In detail

Drop rate is the likelihood an item, tier, or bundle appears when a loot event is resolved. Designers tune drop rates to shape rarity, excitement, and progression pacing while avoiding frustration or inflation.

Synonyms

loot chance, drop chance

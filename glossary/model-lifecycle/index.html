Model Lifecycle

The stages of building, deploying, and maintaining AI models.

In detail

The model lifecycle covers problem framing, data prep, training, validation, deployment, and ongoing monitoring. Clear ownership and feedback loops keep AI systems reliable and aligned to business goals.

Training Data

Training data is the labeled or structured information used to teach AI models. Its quality and coverage strongly influence accuracy, fairness, and reliability.

In detail

Training data is the dataset used to fit AI models to recognize patterns and make predictions. It may be labeled examples for supervised learning or large corpora for self-supervised approaches. High-quality training data should be representative, accurate, and timely. Gaps or bias in training data can reduce performance or lead to unfair outcomes. Good documentation, sampling strategies, and ongoing updates help maintain reliable models as real-world conditions change.

Synonyms

labeled data, ground truth

Evie Rao

Evie is the sort of person who keeps a pocket notebook full of odd phrases, diagrams, and prompt fragments that she tests like recipes. She loves the quiet focus of late-night tinkering, a mug of oolong at her side, and ambient city sounds filtering in from the window. Patterns fascinate her—whether in natural language, subway maps, or the way people explain things when they’re excited. She enjoys puzzle hunts, thrifted mechanical keyboards, and long walks where solutions seem to click three blocks after she stops thinking about them. Friends know her for analog rituals: hand-sketched flowcharts, color-coded tabs, and tiny automation hacks that reduce friction in everyday life. She believes useful tools should feel calm, respectful, and a little playful, and she gravitates toward designs that make complex systems feel like friendly neighborhoods.

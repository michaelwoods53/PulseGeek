Evan Marshall

Evan gravitates toward problems that mix numbers with human behavior, the kind you ponder over a late-night espresso while a dashboard hums quietly in the background. He enjoys tracking market rhythms the way some people follow weather patterns, noticing small shifts that hint at bigger changes. Weekend mornings often find him on a trail run, clearing his head before diving into a new dataset or a fresh idea. He’s the friend who brings a pocket notebook to the farmer’s market, jotting prices and patterns just for the joy of it. Jazz and ambient playlists keep him in flow, and he has a soft spot for analog watches and the patient craft they represent. He cares deeply about practical fairness in technology, believing that clarity and guardrails can help tools serve people instead of the other way around.

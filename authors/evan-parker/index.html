Evan Parker

Evan is the kind of person who sketches process maps on napkins and then turns them into small experiments to see what actually holds up in the real world. He loves the moment when a messy operation becomes transparent through a simple dashboard or a clarified workflow. Weekends often find him trail running at dawn, followed by a meticulous coffee ritual and a stack of sticky notes for new ideas. He mentors a neighborhood robotics club because he enjoys watching curious minds connect cause and effect. Jazz vinyl, well-seasoned cast-iron, and quiet evening walks balance out his penchant for data-heavy problem solving. Above all, he believes technology should reduce friction for people, not add more of it.
